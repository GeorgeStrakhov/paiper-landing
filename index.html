AI-enhanced writing for people who like writing things themselves

Runs on your own device

Paiper runs locally on your own device. No web interfaces. No sign-ins. No notifications. No wifi needed. No token counts. Just you and your best writing, in a minimalistic interface.

Belongs to you, not an evil corp

Your writing process belongs to you. By writing on paiper you make sure that your employer (or the tech company) is not using your process to teach an algorithm that can replace you.

Trained on your favorite writers

Tell us who your favorite writers are or upload your favorite examples of great writing. We will use this information to fine-tune our large language model, so that it reflects your taste. Every paiper is unique.

Learns your style and taste

As you write and rewrite, paiper is learning. Simply select a piece of text and an interface appears that allows you to “love” or “hate” what you selected. You can also ask paiper to suggest ways to rewrite it.
